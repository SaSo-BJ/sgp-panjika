About

This website is a Minimum Viable Product style prototype. The developer has built this tool under the directions of his professor, who is a direct disciple of Śrī Haridāsa Śāstrī Maharāja. The said professor has also assisted in the publication of Śrī Gadādhara Parivāra Panjikā by Śrī Haridāsa Śāstrī Go Sevā Saṁsthāna for the past several years.

The output for Panjikā data is generated via the VedicDateTime package in R and its accuracy has been validated for a fairly large sample.

The Ekādaśī Vrata Sūcī is generated via computations on the Panjikā data (as per the guidelines in Śrī Haribhakti Vilāsa). Its output has been validated and reconciled with Ekādaśī Vrata Sūcī for the years 2019-2025. However, it is still advised to use the Vrata Sūcī only for tallying your computations.

DISCLAIMER(S): For the sake of simplicity, a muhurta has been approximated as 48 minutes to determine the Aruṇodaya timings | The conditions for Viṣṇu Śr̥ṁkhala Yoga have NOT been encoded for Vrata determination | This is a not-for-profit & a non-commercial tool developed solely using free-tier resources.
